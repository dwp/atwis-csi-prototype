Using this service to renew your grant

Civil service employment from 1 April 2022

If your grant for your civil service employment ends on or after 1 April 2022, your employer will provide support instead of Access to Work.

You will not be able to renew your grant for this employment.

You must contact your employer so they can assess and arrange the support you need.

Other employment or self-employment

You can still use this service to renew your Access to Work grant for:

  • self-employment
  • non-civil service employment
  • civil service employment, if your grant end date is before 1 April 2022
