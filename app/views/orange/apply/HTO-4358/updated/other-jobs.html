Using this service to apply for Access to Work

Civil service employment

Your employer will provide support instead of Access to Work.

You will need to contact your employer so they can assess and arrange the support you need.

If you need mental health support

You can get mental health support through Access to Work for all types of jobs, including your civil service employment.

You can apply for mental health support directly from Able Futures or Maximus.

These providers will contact Access to Work on your behalf. You will not need to tell your employer.

Other employment or self-employment

You can still use this service to apply for Access to Work for:

  • self-employment
  • non-civil service employment

Get help

You can contact us for free by:

  • Telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • Textphone: 0800 121 7579
  • Relay UK, if you cannot hear or speak on the phone: 18001 then 0800 121 7479
