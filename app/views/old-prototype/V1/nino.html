What is your National Insurance number?

Why are we asking
Letting us know your National Insurance Number (NINO) speeds up the application process, as we can automatically recover some information with fewer questions. If you would rather not fill this field in, please leave it blank. You will be asked further questions and a DWP advisor will then be in touch.
It’s on your National Insurance card, benefit letter, payslip or P60. For example, ‘QQ 12 34 56 C’.
