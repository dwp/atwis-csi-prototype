New application

  • Your cases and find a case and results (on same screen) - payment person view
  • Reject case, send rejection letter (with option for appointee)
    • prototyped intranet letters page intranet letters (actual page is https://intranet.dwp.gov.uk/section/operational-instructions/customer-products/access-work/letters but only available on Surface Pros)
  • case rejected
  • Case details and change:
    • personal details
    • appointee details and remove appointee
    • company details
    • interview details
  • Your tasks
    • Check on searchlight - unhappy path jumps straight to unhappy path contact options
    • add National Insurance number from Searchlight
    • check for appointee and add appointee details
    • Check personal details (unhappy annotated on screen)
    • company details (unhappy annotated on screen)
    • interview details (unhappy annotated on screen)
    • unhappy path:
      • IF 2 FORMS OF CONTACT: how do you want to contact the applicant? (and option for appointee screen)
      • send email (and option for appointee screen)
      • email sent
      • phone applicant (and option for appointee screen)
      • phoned (and option for appointee screen)
    • Send email (and option for appointee screen)
    • print and send claim form (and option for appointee screen)
      pre-populated PDF form
    • Your cases (waiting), case index (waiting) and claim (waiting)
    • Print and send claim form again and option for appointee

    Same-day application only

    • Your cases (same day)
    • Your tasks (same day) and claim (same day)

    After interview

    • Your cases
    • Your tasks
      • Contact employer
      • Email: Send email and success
      • Telephone: Contact employer and Recorded if able to contact employer
      • Update (goes to confirm)
      • Did the interview happen? and unhappy path:
        • Is the interpreter eligible for a cancellation fee?
          • Yes: Add new amount (continue to tasks complete)
          • No: Do you want to close the case?
            Yes continues to close journey. No:
            • How do you want to contact the applicant? (and option for appointee screen)
            • Email: Contact the applicant (and option for appointee screen) and email sent
            • Phone: Telephone the applicant (and option for appointee screen)
              • and telephone call made
      • Tasks complete
    • Your cases (empty)

    Payments team journey

    • Your cases (simplified) and when allocated
    • Unallocated cases (and empty state)
    • Find a case and results (on same screen) - payment person view
    • Invoice details (locked until added via tasks) and change (page showing all sections unlocked)
    • Tasks to complete: interview hasn't been confirmed and can start but need to allocate to self
    • Tasks to complete
      • Check evidence - unhappy path:
        • ask for more evidence and option for appointee
        • Wait for response and option for appointee
      • Add invoice details
      • Check amount against with quote
      • confirm amount, if amount is higher than quote:
        • Allocate the case to an adviser
        • Your cases (task updated)
      • Find payee on SOP
        • Can't find: Check bank details, if fails request bank details (with option for appointee) and evidence sent (with option for appointee).
      • Check payee details and option if not on list:
        • Fill in the form and task completed
      • Add SOP reference number
      • Check payment details
      • End page
      • Index when payment is complete and recorded cancelled payments

      Adviser journey when invoice is different from payment

      • Your cases
      • Case details
      • Invoice (NEW) - read only
      • Your tasks
        • check difference (unhappy annotated on screen)
        • unhappy path:
          • Do you need to contact the applicant for more details? and option for appointee
          • IF YES:
            • IF 2 FORMS OF CONTACT: how do you want to contact the applicant? (and option for appointee screen)
            • send email (and option for appointee screen)
            • email sent
            • phone applicant (and option for appointee screen)
              • IF YES: goes to what amount is approved below
              • IF NO: phoned (and option for appointee screen)
            • IF NO: What amount is approved?
          • Allocate the case to a payment officer
        • Your cases (empty) - allocated notification
